How to Set Up Your Trezor

Setting up your Trezor is easy and straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Connect your Trezor to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  • Visit trezor.io/start to begin the setup process.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to create your wallet and secure your recovery seed.
